How to Play

  • You start with $1000 and no stocks.
  • Each stock has a price, a rating (chance to rise), and a price change arrow/mini-graph.
  • Buy low, sell high! Try to grow your wealth.
  • Click "Progress 1 Day" to update stock prices and ratings.
  • Specify how many shares to buy when you buy.
  • See stats on your shares' original value, current value, and profit/loss.
  • Occasionally, market events will affect certain stocks!
  • Play the Slot Machine tab for a fun risk to win more cash!
  • Money balance is shared between both games. Try to get rich!
  • Restart anytime with the Restart button.
